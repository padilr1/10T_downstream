Promoter/enhancer ratios

  • Filtered Fantom and Ensembl enhancers found within TKO K36me2 peaks. Ran featureCounts on K27ac bam files for these enhancers.
  • Took promoters defined as 1000 bp upstream and 500 bp downstream. Ran featureCounts on K27ac bam files for these promoters.
  • For each sample, took the sum of K27ac for enhancers, divided by the sum of enhancer lengths, and finally normalized by library depth (CPM). This process was repeat for each sample for promoters. Afterwards, the ratio of promoter to enhancer was calculated.
  • Expectedly, there is greater signal at promoters compared to enhancers
  • Upon losing K36me2, however, (TKO -> QKO) enhancers also lose K27ac, with the promoter/enhancer K27ac ratio increasing in the QKO compared to TKO
